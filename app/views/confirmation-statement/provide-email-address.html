What's the company's registered email address?

In future, we may use this email address to send important information and notices to this company.

This email address will not be available on the Companies House online register.

What will the registered email address be used for?

Information that we might send to this email address includes:

  • reminders to file confirmation statements and accounts
  • warnings about missed filing deadlines
  • information from other government departments that may be crucial to the running of your company (only in exceptional circumstances)

Emails sent to the registered email address are expected to come to the attention of someone acting on behalf of the company.
