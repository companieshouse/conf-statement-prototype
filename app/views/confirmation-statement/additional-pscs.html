An individual person with significant control is someone who owns or controls the company. For example, anyone with more than 25% of voting rights or shares.

A relevant legal entity is a corporate body which, if it were a person, would qualify as a PSC.

An other registrable person (ORP) is a non-corporate entity or office that can legally act as a PSC. An ORP can be a government or government department, a corporation sole, a local authority, or an international organisation whose members include two or more countries, territories or their governments.
