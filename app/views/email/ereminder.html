This is a reminder that you have responsibilities to Companies House. As a director, you're legally responsible for running the company and making sure information is sent to us on time.

You must report certain changes to Companies House within 14 days of them happening. For example, changes to directors, company secretaries, or people with significant control (PSCs).

You must also file a confirmation statement every year, even if there have not been any changes to your company.

There is a new legal requirement to provide a registered email address that Companies House can use to contact the company or someone acting on its behalf. If you have not already provided one, you will be asked to do so when you next file a confirmation statement.

What is a confirmation statement?

A confirmation statement contains details about your company – for example, your registered office address and directors. You must file a confirmation statement every year even if there have not been any changes to your company. This confirms that your records are up to date and the information we hold about your company is correct.

For more information about filing your confirmation statement, go to: gov.uk/running-a-limited-company/company-annual-return
