Incorrect register

Depending on the updates you need to make, you will need to do one of the following before you can file your confirmation statement.

  • register a single alternative inspection location (AD02)
  • move your company's records to the single alternative inspection location (AD03)
  • move your company's records to the registered office (AD04)

Once the register is correct on the Companies House register return to this service to file a confirmation statement.

If the register is correct, go back to the last page and select "Yes".
