Use this service to confirm that company information is up to date, and to submit a confirmation statement (previously known as an annual return) to Companies House.

You can only file a confirmation statement here if the company:

  • is a private limited company
  • has 5 officers or less
  • has 5 shareholders or less

Before filing your confirmation statement, you should check your company's details. You must update your records if any information is incorrect or out of date.

Read the guidance on filing your company's confirmation statement, including how to make changes to your company information.
