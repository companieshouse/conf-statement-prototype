Use this service to confirm that company information is up to date, and to submit a confirmation statement (previously known as an annual return) to Companies House.

You can only file a confirmation statement in this service if the company:

  • is a private limited company
  • has 5 officers or fewer
  • has 5 shareholders or fewer

If the company does not meet all the criteria, you will need to file a confirmation statement using our WebFiling service.

Before filing a confirmation statement, you should check the company's details and update any information that is incorrect or out of date.

Read the guidance on filing a confirmation statement, including how to make changes to your company information. You cannot use this service to make changes to company information.

Filing a confirmation statement costs £13, and you'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account, which is different from a WebFiling account
  • your company number
  • your company authentication code
  • to pay using a credit or debit card, or Companies House account

Your filed confirmation statement will appear on the public record and online.
